FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I qualify for the program?
Eligibility depends on factors like homeownership, property age, current energy systems, and location. Our free assessment evaluates your home and determines what programs and upgrades you qualify for — there's no cost or obligation.
Is the assessment really free?
Yes, the home energy assessment is 100% free with no strings attached. A specialist visits your home, evaluates your current systems, and lets you know what upgrades you qualify for. Nothing is done unless you decide to move forward.
Do I have to pay anything upfront?
Qualified homeowners can receive upgrades with zero upfront cost through the government program. The specifics depend on your eligibility and the upgrades your home qualifies for, which we'll explain during the assessment.
What types of upgrades are covered?
Eligible upgrades include solar panels, HVAC systems, insulation, energy-efficient windows, tankless water heaters, roofing, exterior paint, and landscaping. Most homes qualify for multiple upgrades.
How long does the process take?
The assessment takes about 30–45 minutes. If you qualify, the application is processed within 2–3 weeks. Installation timelines vary by project but most upgrades are completed within 4–6 weeks of approval.
I already have a contractor — why should I use this program?
This gives you a second opinion and lets you know if government programs could help cover costs for your energy upgrades. You may be leaving money on the table without even knowing it.
