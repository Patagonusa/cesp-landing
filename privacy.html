Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

California Energy Savings Program, operated by Patagon Consulting Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you fill out our eligibility form or contact us:

  • Full name (first and last)
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Home address and ZIP code
  • Homeownership status
  • Information about your home energy systems and desired upgrades
  • IP address, browser type, and device information (collected automatically)
  • UTM parameters and advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Click ID)

How We Use Your Information

  • To determine your eligibility for government-funded energy programs
  • To contact you regarding your assessment request via phone, text, or email
  • To schedule and conduct home energy assessments
  • To process applications for energy efficiency programs
  • To improve our website and marketing efforts
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Consent to Communications (TCPA Compliance)

By submitting the eligibility form and checking the consent box, you expressly consent to receive calls and text messages from California Energy Savings Program, Patagon Consulting Inc., and its authorized partners at the phone number you provided. This may include calls and texts made using an automatic telephone dialing system or prerecorded voice messages.

Your consent is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may revoke your consent at any time by replying STOP to any text message, calling us at (888) 480-4286, or emailing info@caenergysavings.com.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Our affiliated companies and authorized service partners for the purpose of completing your home energy assessment and upgrades
  • Government agencies as required for program applications
  • Service providers who help us operate our business (CRM, communication platforms)
  • As required by law, regulation, or legal process

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@caenergysavings.com or call (888) 480-4286.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to analyze website traffic, measure advertising effectiveness, and improve your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
